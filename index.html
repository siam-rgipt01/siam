More About Us

SIAM-RGIPT is the 7th chapter of it's kind in India. IISc Bangalore, IIT Guwahati and DTU Delhi being some of the other institutions where it's already established. SIAM-RGIPT aims to provide the means to the students to excel in the fields that follow an interdisciplinary approach and bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world applications. It provides the students with a platform to brainstorm, freely exchange ideas and work various aspects of mathematics related to their disciplines. It strives to enable the students to push past the boundaries of curriculum and widen their horizons through talks with experts and seasoned professionals, projects, research and industrial exposure.

FAQ

What is SIAM RGIPT?

SIAM RGIPT stands for the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology. It is a student chapter affiliated with SIAM, focusing on promoting applied mathematics and computational science.

Who can join SIAM RGIPT?

Membership is open to all students of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology who have an interest in mathematics, computational science, or related fields.

What are the benefits of joining SIAM RGIPT?

Members can participate in workshops, seminars, and competitions related to applied mathematics and computational science. They also have access to networking opportunities with professionals in the field

How can I become a member of SIAM RGIPT?

To become a member, you can visit our website and fill out the membership form. Membership is typically open to all students of RGIPT.

What activities does SIAM RGIPT organize?

SIAM RGIPT organizes events such as workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and skill development workshops. These events are aimed at enhancing the understanding and application of mathematics in real-world scenarios and related to field.

How can I stay updated about SIAM RGIPT events and activities?

We regularly update our website and social media channels with information about upcoming events. Members also receive email notifications about events and opportunities on regular basis
